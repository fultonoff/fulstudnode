How do we Work?

Our company is constantly simplifying the entry of new candidates to European, Asian and African universities. We help them to achieve their goals and make this experience unique.

1. Student submits us documents, he must include:

  • Passport
  • Diploma(BAC)
  • Exam Marks

Coordinate it with the university. After approval, the student pays the invitation fee(expenses for the invitation form, postage for sending the invitation). After obtaining the invitation.
The procedure can take up to one (1) week.

2.The student applies for a visa. We provide visa support which include:

  • Birth certificate
  • Applicant's valid passport travel document
  • The original letter of invitation issued by the Relevant Ukrainian University / 2 month minimum
  • A photo of the applicant (35 - 45 mm)
  • photocopy of their identity document
  • Copy of the diploma and BAC transcripts legalized at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs./ 6 month minimum
  • A signed visa application form
  • Copy of the birth certificate legalized at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs / 6 minimum.
  • Copy of medical certificate accompanied by a legalized HIV test at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs less than 3 months
  • ORIGINAL Procuration written by the father or mother and the copy of the two-part ID.
  • For the Minor: ORIGINAL Parental authorization of the father and mother legalized to the police, indicating their assent to the expatriation of the child
  • Proof that the applicant has valid travel insurance to cover UKRAINE, COVID-19 and the period of stay (the policy must provide a minimum of 30,000 euros in coverage)
  • The bank account must contain at least $7,000
  • Original of the letter of care made to a notary or the police, by the father the mother the biological brother or sister (if brother or sister presentation of their birth certificate) in case of DECES of the parents it is necessary the CERTIFICATE OF DECES and the copy of the PIECE OF IDENTITY of the person who supports legalized to the police , confirming the coverage of the applicant's expenses in Ukraine, accompanied by the Originals of the bank statements of the last 6 months, booking a round-trip ticket (over one year) or one-way with open return Birth certificate
